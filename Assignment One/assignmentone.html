About Pokemon:


Pokemon is a franchise that was originally created in 1995. The concept of the franchise is to catch small fantasy creatures called Pokemon.
Over the last few years, the franchise has expanded to video games on multiple different consoles such as the Nintendo DS, the Nintendo Switch and the Gameboy. The franchise also has many different TV shows and movies, with new episodes being released constantly.
